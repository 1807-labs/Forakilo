# Code of Conduct

Purpose: define conduct expectations for Forakilo contributors.
Scope: repository discussions, issues, reviews, and project communication.
Audience: contributors, maintainers, and users.
Assumptions: project collaboration should be respectful and evidence-driven.
Dependencies: [Contributing](CONTRIBUTING.md), [Support](SUPPORT.md).
Unresolved decisions: formal enforcement committee and contact channel.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and precise.
- Challenge claims with evidence rather than personal attacks.
- Treat financial, security, privacy, and legal risks seriously.
- Welcome correction when documentation or assumptions are wrong.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, disclosure of private information, posting secrets, encouraging unsafe trading behavior, or pressuring maintainers to bypass safety gates is unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or restrict contributions that violate this policy. A private reporting channel should be configured by the repository owner before public community launch.
