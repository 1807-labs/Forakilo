# Legal Review Checklist

This is a working technical draft for product planning and is not a substitute for qualified legal advice.

Purpose: identify legal review items before launch or scope expansion.
Scope: Canada-first MVP, controlled beta, and jurisdiction expansion.
Audience: legal counsel, product owners, risk reviewers, and maintainers.
Assumptions: no legal permission is inferred from technical documentation.
Dependencies: [Product Classification](PRODUCT_CLASSIFICATION_AND_REGULATORY_ASSUMPTIONS.md), [Jurisdiction Research](JURISDICTION_RESEARCH.md).
Unresolved decisions: all items require qualified review.

## Checklist

- Product classification by feature and jurisdiction.
- Privacy law obligations, consent, breach, access, deletion, retention, and subprocessors.
- Crypto asset, securities, derivatives, FX/CFD, automated trading, and investment-advice analysis.
- Provider account terms, API terms, data licensing, caching, redistribution, and geographic restrictions.
- Disclosures, risk acknowledgements, terms, acceptable use, and support process.
- Live trading eligibility, capital/risk limits, user suitability, and manual approvals.
- Incident notification and regulatory reporting obligations.
- Marketing claims and prohibited performance language.
- Tax, recordkeeping, and audit evidence obligations.
- Accessibility obligations and consumer protection review.
