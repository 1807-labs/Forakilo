# Product Classification and Regulatory Assumptions

This is a working technical draft for product planning and is not a substitute for qualified legal advice.

Purpose: define assumed operating model and regulatory boundaries.
Scope: Canada-first MVP and expansion framework.
Audience: product owners, legal counsel, risk reviewers, and engineers.
Assumptions: Forakilo does not hold user funds, accept deposits, process withdrawals, pool assets, or provide personalized financial advice in the MVP.
Dependencies: [Jurisdiction Research](JURISDICTION_RESEARCH.md), [Risk Disclosure](RISK_DISCLOSURE.md).
Unresolved decisions: final legal classification and registrations require counsel.

## Assumed MVP Classification

Forakilo is scoped as a multi-user analytics and paper-trading platform with possible later user-authorized third-party execution after gates. It is not assumed to be legally permitted to operate as an investment adviser, portfolio manager, broker, dealer, custodian, money-service business, marketplace, or copy-trading service.

## MVP Boundaries

- Users retain accounts with approved third-party brokers or exchanges.
- Forakilo does not custody fiat or cryptocurrency.
- Forakilo does not accept deposits or process withdrawals.
- Forakilo does not pool user funds.
- Forakilo does not promise returns.
- Forakilo does not provide personalized financial advice.
- Forakilo does not enable social or copy trading in MVP.
- Exchange credentials exclude withdrawal permission.
- Paper trading precedes live trading.
- Live trading requires explicit user authorization and risk acknowledgement.

## Legal Review Triggers

Any move toward live trading, personalized recommendations, copy trading, custody, margin/leverage, derivatives, stablecoins/value-referenced crypto assets, paid signals, managed accounts, or non-Canadian users MUST trigger legal review.
