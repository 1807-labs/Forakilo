# Terms of Service Draft

This is a working technical draft for product planning and is not a substitute for qualified legal advice.

Purpose: outline terms needed before user-facing service launch.
Scope: account use, prohibited use, risks, providers, data, models, and limitations.
Audience: product owners, legal counsel, and maintainers.
Assumptions: no public service is currently operated.
Dependencies: [Risk Disclosure](RISK_DISCLOSURE.md), [Privacy Policy Draft](PRIVACY_POLICY_DRAFT.md).
Unresolved decisions: governing law, dispute process, commercial terms, support commitments, and limitation language.

## Draft Sections

- Eligibility and account registration.
- Authentication, account security, and credential responsibilities.
- Prohibited use, including illegal activity, market manipulation, unauthorized access, and credential abuse.
- Third-party providers and user responsibility for provider accounts.
- Paper trading limitations.
- Live trading gates and user authorization if later enabled.
- Model and data limitations.
- No personalized financial advice in MVP.
- Fees and taxes if applicable.
- Privacy and data handling.
- Suspension and termination.
- Disclaimers, limitation of liability, and indemnity subject to legal review.
- Changes to terms and notice.
