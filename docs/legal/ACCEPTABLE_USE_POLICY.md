# Acceptable Use Policy

This is a working technical draft for product planning and is not a substitute for qualified legal advice.

Purpose: define unacceptable uses of Forakilo.
Scope: user conduct, provider access, market conduct, platform abuse, and security abuse.
Audience: users, product owners, support, legal counsel, and security reviewers.
Assumptions: final enforceability requires legal review.
Dependencies: [Terms Draft](TERMS_OF_SERVICE_DRAFT.md), [Abuse Controls](../security/ABUSE_AND_FRAUD_CONTROLS.md).
Unresolved decisions: enforcement process and appeal path.

## Prohibited Uses

Users MUST NOT use Forakilo to:

- Violate laws, regulations, provider terms, or market rules.
- Attempt market manipulation, wash trading, spoofing, layering, or abusive order behavior.
- Share, sell, or transfer accounts or credentials without authorization.
- Circumvent geographic, provider, risk, or eligibility restrictions.
- Upload malicious code, attack the platform, scrape without authorization, or evade rate limits.
- Store withdrawal-enabled provider credentials in Forakilo.
- Present Forakilo outputs as personalized financial advice.
